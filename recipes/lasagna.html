Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide flat pasta sheets, alternating with rich and savory ingredients such as Bolognese sauce, b√©chamel sauce, and cheese. The layers are baked to perfection, resulting in a hearty and flavorful casserole-style dish. It's a popular comfort food enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
